The project contains all required files:
1. index.html - Entry point with Tailwind and Lucide.
2. styles.css - Custom styling for logo and glass effects.
3. main.js - Routing and initialization.
4. views.js - Template generation for views.
5. data_store.js - Mock data.
6. logo_component.js - Specific logo rendering.

The design strictly follows the user goal:
- Logo: "PROJECT" block white font, "Los Santos" cursive olive font.
- Theme: Dark, modern, gaming aesthetic with olive accents.
- Pages: Home, Forum, Store, Rules.
- Navbar: Fixed, includes Discord link.
- Tech: Vanilla JS, Tailwind, Lucide.
