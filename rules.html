SERVER RULES

Follow these guidelines to ensure fair and enjoyable roleplay for everyone

General Rules

1

No Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Killing without valid in-character reason or initiation is strictly prohibited. All combat requires proper roleplay buildup.

2

No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

Using vehicles as weapons without roleplay reason is not allowed. This includes intentionally running over players.

3

Metagaming Prohibited

Using out-of-character information in-character is forbidden. This includes stream sniping and Discord communication during active RP.

4

Powergaming

Forcing actions on other players without giving them a chance to respond (/me attempts to... not /me does...).

Roleplay Guidelines

1

Character Creation

Create realistic characters with depth. No celebrities, superheroes, or unrealistic backgrounds. Characters must have flaws and vulnerabilities.

2

Fear Roleplay

Your character must show appropriate fear when threatened with weapons or superior force. Value your character's life at all times.

3

New Life Rule

If you are downed and respawn, you forget the immediate events leading to your death. You cannot return to the same scenario for 15 minutes.

4

Breaking Character

Do not break character unless explicitly instructed by staff. Use /ooc for out-of-character communication only when necessary.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server?

Download FiveM from the official website, launch the application, search for "Project Los Santos" in the server browser, or direct connect using the IP: play.projectlossantos.com

Do I need to apply for whitelist?

Currently we offer both public and whitelist slots. Public slots are available immediately, while whitelist slots require an application for priority queue access and certain restricted jobs.

What are the server restart times?

Server restarts occur every 6 hours at: 00:00, 06:00, 12:00, and 18:00 EST. Each restart is announced 15 minutes, 5 minutes, and 1 minute prior.

How do I report a bug or player?

Use our Forum's Bug Reports or Support sections. For urgent player reports, use the in-game /report command or contact a staff member via Discord ticket.
